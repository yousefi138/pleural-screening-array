Overview

This document summarises the versioned DNAm data release for the pleural cfDNA screening array study (project M2644). It links array locations to the released samplesheet, constructs a combined phenotype table annotating each sample with sex, malignancy status, plate, and QC-pass/fail status, and produces a stratified descriptive table to characterise the study population.

Sample Characteristics of QC Pass/Fail Samples

Of 336 samples, 28 (8.3%) failed QC and were excluded from the clean release. CpG Site QC

Of 284,317 CpG sites on the assay, 48,216 (17%) did not pass QC and are absent from the released beta-value matrix, leaving 236,101 sites available for analysis.

Sample Correlation Structure

To assess the overall similarity between samples, pairwise Pearson correlations were computed across all 236,101 post-QC CpG sites. The histogram below shows the distribution of all 47,278 upper-triangle sample-pair correlations, with a density overlay.
